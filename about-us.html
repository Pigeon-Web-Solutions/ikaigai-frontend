Introduction to IKIGAIYour Pathway to Japan

Ikigai Japanese Language School is a leading Japanese language and SSW preparation institute in Mahendranagar, Nepal, dedicated to helping students achieve their goals of studying and working in Japan.

We offer Japanese language courses from N5 to N3, along with JFT Basic and specialised skill test preparation required for SSW, Trainee (TITP), Student, Dependent, and Gijinkoku visas. Guided by experienced instructors, we provide structured learning, practical exam strategies, and personalised support.
